About

A simple website to help you better understand which side of the street to be parked on in Syracuse, NY.

Syracuse uses odd even parking, or alternative-side parking, to make sure snowplows and street-sweepers have access on narrow roads.

This website is unofficial and not related in any manner to the city government of Syracuse. For more information, visit Syracuse's Parking Rules page.

If you find something wrong or have a suggestion, email info@oddeven.org or do a pull request on GitHub.

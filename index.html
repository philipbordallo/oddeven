About This Site

This is a simple website to help you better understand which side of the street to be parked on in Syracuse, NY. The city of Syracuse uses odd even parking, or alternative-side parking, to make sure snowplows and street-sweepers have access on narrow roads.

For more information, visit Syracuse's Parking FAQ.

This website is unofficial and not related in any manner to the city governemnt of Syracuse, NY. If you find something wrong or have a suggestion, email info@oddeven.org or do a pull request on GitHub.
